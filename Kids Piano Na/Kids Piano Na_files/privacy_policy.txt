Privacy Policy for Kids Piano Na
Last updated: 26.02.2024

Welcome to Kids Piano Na, developed by Iurii Sevstianov. Our commitment to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children, is paramount. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information for anyone who uses our app.

1. Information Collection and Use
For a better experience while using our Service, we wish to inform you that we do not collect any personal information from our users. Kids Piano Na is designed to be a safe and secure app for children, with a focus on providing an enjoyable musical experience without compromising privacy.

2. No Advertising ID Use
We understand the concerns regarding targeted advertising and user tracking, especially in apps designed for children. Kids Piano Na does not use any Advertising ID or similar mechanisms for tracking purposes. Our app is free from advertisements, ensuring a safe and uninterrupted experience for our users.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at music.aglince@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy page was created to assure the safety and privacy of all users of Kids Piano Na, reflecting our commitment to ensuring a secure environment for children.
